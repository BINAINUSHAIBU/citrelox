About us?

DR. HERBERT OYE External Member of Council Dr. Oye graduated from the Univ of North Texas Health Science Center Texas College of Osteopathic Med in 1991. He works in Beckley, WV and specializes in General Surgery and Vascular Surgery. Dr. Oye is affiliated with Raleigh General Hospital and Beckley ARH Hospital. Dr. Herbert Oye is a Board Certified Endovascular and Vascular Surgeon, specializing in invasive and non-invasive treatment of vascular diseases. His General Surgery residence was performed at Wyckoff Heights Medical Center in Brooklyn, NY, an affiliate of Albert Einstein Medical Center. Dr. Oye completed his fellowship training in advanced vascular and endovascular surgery at the Arizona Heart Institute in Phoenix, Arizona.
